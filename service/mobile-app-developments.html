Native App Development: • Built specifically for iOS (Swift/SwiftUI) or Android (Kotlin/Java) • Optimal performance and platform-specific features • Best for apps requiring high performance, complex animations, or deep hardware integration • Requires separate development teams for each platform Cross-Platform Development (Flutter/React Native): • Single codebase for both iOS and Android • 30-40% reduction in development time and cost • Near-native performance with hot reload for faster development • Easier maintenance with unified codebase The choice depends on your budget, timeline, and performance requirements. For most business apps, cross-platform offers excellent value. For gaming or apps requiring intensive device features, native may be preferred. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
