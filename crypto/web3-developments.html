Understanding Web3

What Is Web3
Development?

Web3 development involves building decentralized applications (dApps) and platforms that operate on blockchain networks, giving users control over their data, assets, and interactions without intermediaries.

Web3 solutions include decentralized finance (DeFi) apps, NFT marketplaces, token systems, identity solutions, and other blockchain-powered applications that are secure, trustless, and transparent.
